## Section 2 — Building Layout and Floor Directory

### 2.1 Basement Level (B1) — Infrastructure & Parking

The basement level is a restricted-access floor. Entry requires a **Blue-tier badge** (Infrastructure, IT, and Security teams only) or an escorted visitor pass issued at the Security Desk on Floor 1.

### 3.3 Room Booking System

All meeting rooms (except the Boardroom and HR private rooms) are booked through **Google Calendar** using the room's resource calendar.

#### Booking via Google Calendar

1. Open Google Calendar and create a new event.
2. In the **Guests** field, type the room name (e.g., `Yosemite`) — the room resource calendar will auto-suggest.
3. Select the room. You will see the room's current availability overlaid on your event.
4. Send the invite. You will receive an automatic confirmation if the room is available, or a decline if there is a conflict.
5. Rooms are held for **10 minutes** after the scheduled start time. If the meeting has not started (door not unlocked via badge), the room is automatically released back to availability.

#### Booking via the Facilities Portal

For complex setups (catering, specific A/V configurations, room rearrangement) or multi-day bookings:

1. Navigate to `portal.techcorp.io > Facilities > Room Booking`.
2. Select the floor, room name, date, and time.
3. Add special requirements in the notes field (e.g., "Configure for workshop: remove central table, arrange chairs in circle, whiteboard markers needed").
4. Submit. Facilities will confirm within 4 business hours for standard requests, 8 hours for complex setups.

#### Room Booking Policies

- **Advance booking limit**: Rooms may be booked up to **4 weeks** in advance. Exceptions for quarterly planning or All-Hands require Facilities approval.
- **Maximum single booking duration**: 4 consecutive hours for standard rooms. Extensions require Facilities approval.
- **Recurring bookings**: Permitted for standing team meetings of up to 1 hour, up to 3 months at a time. Recurring bookings must be reviewed and re-confirmed each quarter.
- **Cancellation**: Cancel at least **2 hours before** the start time to free the room for others. Rooms left booked but unused ("ghost meetings") are tracked. Teams with more than 3 ghost meetings in a calendar month will have their advance booking window reduced to 2 weeks.
- **Room capacity**: Do not book a 4-person room for 12 attendees. Security regulations limit occupancy to the posted room capacity. Occupancy limits are posted on each room's door.
- **Food and drink**: Drinks with lids are permitted in all rooms. Full catering is permitted in rooms TC-301 (Olympic) and TC-401 (Grand Canyon) and the Boardroom only. Arrange catering via `facilities@techcorp.io` at least 24 hours in advance.

### 3.4 A/V and Technology Support in Rooms

All rooms with a display support **wireless presentation** via Apple AirPlay or the HDMI cable coiled on the table. The room display automatically wakes when a laptop is connected or an AirPlay session is initiated.

**Video Conferencing**: Rooms equipped with video conferencing have a dedicated Google Meet hardware unit (Logitech Rally or Poly Studio) pre-configured. To start a call:
1. Tap the touchscreen panel on the conference table.
2. Select **Join Google Meet** — it will auto-populate with any Google Calendar event scheduled in this room.
3. Alternatively, enter the meeting code manually.

**A/V Issues**: Contact IT Help Desk (Floor 3) via `#it-help` on Slack or call the A/V hotline: ext. 5555. For urgent A/V issues during a meeting, press the **red call button** on the room's touchscreen panel for immediate remote support.

## Section 4 — Building Access and Physical Security

### 4.1 ID Badge Overview

All employees, contractors, and long-term vendors are issued a **TechCorp proximity access badge** (HID Seos format). The badge doubles as your building access card and your identity credential at all internal access points.

Badges are color-coded by tier:

| Badge Color | Tier | Standard Access |
|---|---|---|
| **White** | Standard Employee | Lobby, Floors 1–4, Cafeteria, Bike Storage |
| **Green** | Contractor / Vendor | Lobby, Floor 1, Cafeteria, and specific floors as noted on badge |
| **Blue** | Infrastructure / IT / Security | All of the above + Basement (B1), Server Room (requires dual-factor entry) |
| **Gold** | Executive (VP+) | All floors, including Floor 5 Executive Suite and Boardroom |
| **Red** | Temporary / Visitor | Lobby and Floor 1 only, with escort required for all other floors |

#### Badge Issuance

New employees receive their badge during the Day 1 onboarding session with People Ops. The badge is pre-programmed for the correct tier based on your role.

#### Lost or Forgotten Badge

- **Lost badge**: Report immediately to `security@techcorp.io` or the Security Desk (Floor 1). The lost badge is deactivated within **15 minutes** of the report. A replacement is issued within 2 business days. There is a **$25 replacement fee** for the second and subsequent lost badges within a 12-month period; the first replacement is free.
- **Forgotten badge**: Show government-issued photo ID at the Security Desk. A single-day temporary badge (Red tier) will be issued. You must be escorted by a badged colleague to access any floor above Floor 1. Temporary badges must be returned at the end of the business day.

#### Badge Etiquette — Anti-Tailgating Policy

**Tailgating** (allowing an unbadged person to enter through an access-controlled door behind you) is a serious security violation. It is prohibited without exception, even for colleagues you recognize.

- If someone behind you does not badge in, politely direct them to the Security Desk or use the intercom adjacent to the door.
- Security cameras monitor all badge-controlled access points. Tailgating incidents are flagged automatically by the physical security system (Lenel S2) and reviewed by the Security team.
- A first tailgating offense results in a formal security warning. A second offense may result in badge suspension and disciplinary action.

### 4.2 After-Hours Access

The building is accessible 24/7 to badged employees. However, the following restrictions apply outside standard business hours (Monday–Friday, 7:00 AM–9:00 PM):

- **Lobby is unmanned** but security cameras and the remote security monitoring service remain active.
- **Cafeteria is closed**. Vending machines on Floors 2 and 4 are available after hours.
- **IT Help Desk is closed**. After-hours hardware or access emergencies: `it-oncall@techcorp.io`.
- **Guests and unescorted visitors are not permitted** after hours. A badged employee who brings an after-hours guest must register the visit via the After-Hours Visitor Registration form in the Facilities portal at least 2 hours before arrival and must remain with the guest at all times.
- **Solo after-hours work protocol**: Employees working alone in the building after 10:00 PM are encouraged to notify the remote security monitoring team at `security-monitoring@techcorp.io` as a safety check-in. This is voluntary but recommended.

### 4.3 Visitor and Guest Registration

All visitors (clients, candidates, vendors, family members) must be registered before arriving.

#### Pre-Registering a Visitor

1. Navigate to `portal.techcorp.io > Facilities > Visitor Registration` or use the Envoy app.
2. Enter the visitor's full name, company, purpose of visit, and expected arrival time.
3. The visitor will receive an automated email with directions, a QR code, and a reminder 2 hours before arrival.
4. On arrival, the visitor scans the QR code at the Envoy kiosk in the lobby. Their photo is taken and a printed Red-tier visitor badge is issued automatically.
5. The host employee receives a Slack notification the moment the visitor checks in and must meet them in the lobby within **5 minutes**. Visitors may not proceed past the lobby unescorted.

#### Walk-In Visitors

Unregistered visitors are accepted at the discretion of the Security Desk. The host employee will be called and must come to the lobby to vouch for the visitor and escort them throughout the visit. Walk-in visitors cause delays for the host; pre-registration is always preferred.

#### Visitor Escort Requirements

Visitors must be accompanied by a badged employee at all times above Floor 1. Visitors may never be left alone in meeting rooms, engineering areas, or any space with visible screens or whiteboards containing confidential information. Before leaving a visitor in a meeting room to retrieve equipment or coffee, clear whiteboards and lock laptop screens.

#### NDAs and Confidentiality

Visitors whose visit involves exposure to non-public product information, source code, or client data must sign an NDA before entry. The NDA workflow is managed via DocuSign; initiate it through `legal@techcorp.io` at least **24 hours before** the visit.

### 4.4 Parking Garage Access

The basement parking garage has 84 reserved spaces. Parking allocation is managed by Facilities and follows this priority order:

1. Executive team (C-suite and VPs): 14 permanently reserved spots (B1, Row A)
2. Employees with documented medical accommodation: allocated on request via `accommodations@techcorp.io`
3. Directors and Managers: entered into the waiting list; spaces allocated as they become available

- On arrival, the ANPR camera reads the plate and opens the gate. No badge tap required for vehicles.
- **Guest parking**: 6 spaces in B1 Row G are reserved for pre-registered guests. Book guest parking via the Visitor Registration form in the Facilities portal. Guest parking is complimentary for client visits only; job candidates and vendors cover their own parking costs.
- **EV Charging**: 12 Level 2 EV charging stations (ChargePoint) are available in B1, Rows D and E. TechCorp covers charging costs for employees. App download and account registration: `chargepoint.com/techcorp`. Report charging station malfunctions to Facilities.

### 5.2 Fire Evacuation Procedure

In the event of a fire alarm:

1. **Stop work immediately.** Do not wait to save files or gather belongings.
2. **Locate the nearest fire exit stairwell.** Exit maps are posted inside every meeting room and at every elevator lobby on each floor. Do **not** use elevators during an evacuation.
3. **Proceed to your floor's designated Assembly Point** (see Section 5.3).
4. **Floor Wardens** (identified by orange high-visibility vests kept in the emergency cabinets near each stairwell) will confirm all occupants have evacuated. Every employee must check in with their floor warden at the assembly point. Do not assume someone else has been accounted for.
5. Do not re-enter the building until the **All Clear** is announced by San Francisco Fire Department (SFFD) personnel or the Building Management team.

### 5.4 Shelter-in-Place and Lockdown Procedures

In the event of an active threat, hazardous material incident, or severe weather event requiring shelter-in-place:

1. An emergency notification will be broadcast via the **PA system**, **Slack #emergency-alerts channel**, and **automated SMS** to all employees registered in the Emergency Notification System (ENS).
2. Move away from windows and exterior glass. Proceed to an interior room or corridor.
3. Lock or barricade the door if an active threat is involved.
4. Silence mobile phones (vibrate only). Do not open doors for anyone until an All Clear is issued by SFFD or TechCorp Security.
5. Account for all persons in your immediate area and report any injured or missing persons via text to the Security Desk: +1 (415) 555-0110.

**Emergency Notification System Registration**: All employees must register their personal mobile number in the ENS within their first week. ## Section 7 — Facilities Requests and Maintenance

### 7.1 Submitting a Facilities Ticket

For maintenance issues, custodial requests, temperature complaints, broken equipment, and room setup requests:

1. Navigate to `servicenow.techcorp.io > Facilities & Real Estate > Submit Request`.
2. Select the appropriate category: Maintenance, Cleaning, Temperature/HVAC, Furniture, A/V Equipment, or Catering.
3. Provide the room number or desk location, a description of the issue, and any photos (strongly encouraged for maintenance issues).

**Priority levels:**

| Priority | Examples | Target Response |

| P3 — Standard | Furniture request, temperature adjustment, vending restock | 1 business day |
| P4 — Low | Aesthetic issues, minor maintenance, supply requests | 3 business days |
